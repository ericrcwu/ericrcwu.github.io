Minecraft

Minecraft is a game about placing blocks and going on adventures. Explore randomly generated worlds and build amazing things from the simplest of homes to the grandest of castles. Play in Creative Mode with unlimited resources or mine deep in Survival Mode, crafting weapons and armor to fend off dangerous mobs. Do all this alone or with friends.

Mojang

They were founded in 2009 by Markus “Notch” Persson. He’s also the creator of our best-selling game to date: Minecraft. Since then, they’ve released a second game, the card-collecting tactical battler Scrolls, and have dabbled in publishing with Oxeye Game Studio’s awesome side-scrolling robo-blaster Cobalt. They’re developing more games, too, but we’re not ready to talk about those quite yet. In 2014, Mojang was bought by Microsoft. The acquisition hasn’t changed much about our company - they still release and support their games on multiple platforms, including iOS, Playstation and more, as well as Windows and Xbox. they plan to keep our independent spirit and they’ll continue to treat our community with the respect and honesty it deserves.
