RULES

For this game, you have to use iMac otherwise the game won't work. Also
the tab has to be in full screen mode. The game
is simple, go through the waves of zombies and survive. You
go through 30 waves and you win. Each zombie,
you kill gives you money, in the first round you get $5 per
kill, in the second round you get $10 per kill, in the third... and so on. You can get gun upgrades, armour upgrades, and health upgrades. Each
wave will be harder, the zombies get upgrades, more health,
more speed, and better weapons.





Good Luck.
